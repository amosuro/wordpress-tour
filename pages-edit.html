1. Editing a Page

    This is the Edit Page screen. You'll very quickly become familiar with this section, since it's usually the pages on your site that need the most maintenance!

  2. Since there are a number of panels here that are featured in the Posts section, we'll only focus on the differences

  3. On this particular page, there's a notice explaining that Wordpress has an autosave version of the page. This usually occurs if you were editing a page's content and quit the browser unexpectedly. Wordpress tries to recover what you were working on!

  4. Page Attributes is a new panel that is exclusive to the Edit Page section. It allows you to assign a different page layout (if applicable) to the page. In most cases, you won't need to edit this at all.

  5. Whilst the Featured Image panel appears on the Posts section, in this particular case the Featured Image could relate to a number of different parts of the page depending on your website's design. E.g. on some sites it may control the header image used on that particular page
